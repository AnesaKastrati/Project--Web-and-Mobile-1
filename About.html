About Ulpiana Cars

Your trusted partner in premium vehicle rentals.

Who We Are

Ulpiana Cars is a premium car rental company dedicated to providing high–quality vehicles, unbeatable service, and a seamless rental experience. Our fleet includes luxury sedans, SUVs, convertibles, and specialized vehicles to suit every customer's needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — reliable service, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer care. We strive to make every journey smooth, comfortable, and unforgettable.

Our Services

We offer a variety of rental solutions designed to fit any lifestyle or travel purpose:

  • • Long-term vehicle rental
  • • Short-term & weekend rental
  • • Professional chauffeur service
  • • Airport pick-up & drop-off
  • • Premium insurance protection
  • • Luxury package rentals
  • • 24/7 roadside assistance
